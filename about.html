About Oculis Iuris

Oculis Iuris — "In the Eyes of Law" — is the official Forensic Medicine club of JSS Medical College, Mysore. We are a community of curious medical minds united by a passion for forensic science, medico-legal principles, and the pursuit of justice through medicine.

We bridge the gap between clinical knowledge and legal application, equipping future doctors with the skills to navigate court testimonies, examine evidence, understand thanatology, and contribute meaningfully to the medico-legal system of India.

From hands-on case discussions to toxicology workshops and crime scene simulations, Oculis Iuris creates a space where forensic science comes alive.
